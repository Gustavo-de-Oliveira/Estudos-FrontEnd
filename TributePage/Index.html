Linha do tempo da vida de Aurora Aksnes:

  • 1996 - 15/06 in Stavanger, Norway
  • She began creating melodies at the age of 6, and began writing lyrics at the age of 9. Songs such as "Running With the Wolves", "I Went Too Far" and "Runaway" were written in this early stage of her life, "Runaway" being written when she was 12.
  • 2012 - "Puppet"
  • 2013 - "Awakening"
  • 2014 - "Under Stars"
  • 2015 - "Runaway" - "Running with the Wolves" - "Murder Song (5, 4, 3, 2, 1)" - "Half the World Away"
  • 2016 - "Conqueror" - "I Went Too Far" - "Winter Bird"
  • 2018 - "Queendom" - "Forgotten Love"
  • 2019 - "Animal"
